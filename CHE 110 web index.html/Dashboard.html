Let's Look into Lpu









Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a private university located in Chaheru, Phagwara, Punjab, India. It was established in 2005 by Lovely International Trust, under The Lovely Professional University Act, 2005 (Punjab Act 25 of 2005) and started operation in 2006. LPU is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, world-class facilities, and excellent academic programs. The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields such as engineering, management, law, architecture, design, pharmacy, and more.
LPU is also known for its innovative teaching methods, which include experiential learning, industry collaborations, and international exposure. The university has a diverse student community, with students from over 50 countries studying on campus. Additionally, LPU is the only college in all of Punjab that runs on an electricity hotline, averaging 126 Kilowatts of electricity per day, with incredible generator backup systems that ensure power cuts do not last more than five seconds.

LPU has more than 20 schools that provide over 200 programs in engineering, technology, management, pharmacy, social science, and hotel management[3]. The university offers courses in various fields such as engineering, management, law, architecture, design, agriculture, and many more[4]. LPU has over 360 programs at the undergraduate, postgraduate, doctoral, and diploma levels[5]. The university is affiliated with the University Grants Commission, the National Council for Teacher Education, and the Council of Architecture, and approved by the Pharmacy Council of India[4].
